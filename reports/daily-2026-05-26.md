### ASQA Latest News

**Suspension on new CRICOS applications**
*Published: 2026-05-19 | Relevance: HIGH*

The Australian Government introduced a temporary suspension on certain new applications under the ESOS Act, covering CRICOS registration for new providers and course additions. ASQA published the notice on 19 May 2026. RTOs currently CRICOS-registered are not affected in their existing operations, but any provider planning to submit new CRICOS applications or expand ESOS-covered course offerings should pause those plans until the suspension is lifted. No end date has been confirmed by the government.

Content-worthy: YES

### ITECA News

**National Update — Why students need to matter more than institutions**
*Published: 2026-05-25 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

ITECA's latest national update argues that government policy is prioritising institutional interests over student outcomes, at a time when the newly-established Australian Tertiary Education Commission (ATEC) is consolidating oversight of both VET and higher education. RTOs operating across both sectors should watch how this centralisation debate shapes future funding and enrolment policy.

**Government sidelines regulator using own bent regulatory stick**
*Published: 2026-05-18 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

ITECA reports the Australian Government restricted innovation in international education by circumventing the regulator, applying direct policy pressure outside formal TEQSA/ASQA channels. RTOs with CRICOS registration should note this: if government decisions on international education are being made outside standard regulatory frameworks, compliance planning and enrolment forecasting for CRICOS providers becomes harder to anchor to regulator guidance alone.

Content-worthy: YES
LinkedIn angle: The government is making calls on international education without going through the regulator — if you're a CRICOS provider, that matters more than you might think.

URL: https://www.iteca.edu.au/news/international/International_2026/Government_sidelines_regulator__using_own_bent_regulatory_stick.aspx
