### ASQA Media Releases

**Tribunal decision affirms cancellation of Gills College qualifications**
*Published: 2026-05-27 | Relevance: HIGH*

The Administrative Review Tribunal has upheld ASQA's earlier decision to cancel qualifications issued by Gills College. This is the latest in a series of tribunal decisions affirming ASQA's authority to cancel qualifications from non-compliant providers. RTOs should note that once ASQA cancels qualifications and the decision survives tribunal review, there is no further avenue for the provider to continue delivering those qualifications — and the reputational and operational consequences are permanent.
