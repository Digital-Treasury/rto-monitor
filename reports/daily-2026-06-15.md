### ASQA Latest News

**2026 ASQA Provider and Course Owner Survey closing date extended**
*Published: 2026-06-15 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

ASQA has extended the deadline for its 2026 Provider and Course Owner Survey to midnight Friday 19 June 2026. The survey collects provider feedback on ASQA's performance during the 2025–26 financial year, covering sector-wide organisational risks and corporate measures. Original invitations were sent by research partner ORIMA on 19 May 2026 — providers who missed it now have a second chance.

### training.gov.au

**Issue with Training Package Release notification emails and the website Compare Tool**
*Published: date unclear | Relevance: MEDIUM*

Training.gov.au is currently investigating a bug where 'Changes to Training Package' notification emails and the Compare Tool are incorrectly listing superseded units as removed from the wrong training package release. RTOs relying on these notifications or the Compare Tool to track which units changed between releases may be receiving inaccurate information. Developers are working to resolve the issue as a priority.

Content-worthy: NO
