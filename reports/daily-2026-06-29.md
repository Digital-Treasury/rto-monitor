### ASQA Latest News

**Cost Recovery Implementation Statement 2026–27 now available**
*Published: 2026-06-29 | Relevance: HIGH*

ASQA has published its Cost Recovery Implementation Statement (CRIS) for 2026–27, detailing all fees and charges that will apply to regulatory activities from 1 July 2026. This affects all RTOs and CRICOS providers — any registration, renewal, or audit activity triggered on or after 1 July will be subject to the updated schedule. RTOs with pending regulatory interactions should review the CRIS before the new financial year begins.

**ASQA's revised Regulatory Assessment and Monitoring Approach now available**
*Published: 2026-06-26 | Relevance: HIGH*

ASQA has released a revised version of its Regulatory Assessment and Monitoring Approach, explaining how the regulator undertakes risk-based regulation to support quality outcomes and VET integrity. This is a foundational document that shapes how audits are scoped, how risk is weighted, and how ongoing monitoring is conducted. RTOs should read this to understand what ASQA is looking for and how it will prioritise regulatory attention.

### Jobs and Skills Australia

**Partial VET completion delivers jobs and pay boost**
*Published: 2026-06-26 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

JSA research finds that students who partially complete VET qualifications still gain purposeful, job-relevant skills that support entry into work. The study provides evidence that partial completions are not simply dropouts — they can represent deliberate skill-stacking. Relevant for RTOs managing withdrawal data and communicating outcomes to prospective students and employers.
