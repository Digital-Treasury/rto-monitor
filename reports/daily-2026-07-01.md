### ITECA News

**Government Delivers Unwelcome Visa Fee Surprise**
*Published: 2026-07-01 | Relevance: HIGH*

Visa application charges (VACs) for all Australian visas changed on 1 July 2026. ITECA has flagged this as an unwelcome surprise for education providers. CRICOS-registered RTOs that enrol international students are required to provide accurate fee information to prospective students — the changed VACs mean any pre-existing cost guides, enrolment documentation or website content quoting visa fees will now be outdated. RTOs should verify current charges via the Home Affairs fees and charges page and update student-facing materials promptly.

### Jobs and Skills Australia

**Australian Jobs Report 2026: Exploring pathways in a changing world of work**
*Published: 2026-06-30 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

JSA has released the 2026 edition of Australian Jobs, its annual labour market report now in its 30th year. The report maps where employment opportunities exist, identifies emerging and transferable skills, and examines the potential impact of generative AI on jobs and workplaces. For RTOs, the report is a useful reference for assessing whether current course offerings align with where employer skill demand is heading. It also covers multiple entry points into careers, which supports the case for VET pathways alongside university.

Content-worthy: YES
LinkedIn angle: The Australian Jobs 2026 report is out — it maps which skills and occupations are growing and where AI is reshaping work, useful reading for any RTO reviewing its training mix.
