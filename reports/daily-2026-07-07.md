**Regional, Rural and Remote VET Policy Decision Making Project**
*Published: 2026-07-07 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

Skills Insight has launched a feasibility study into how VET decision-making data can better serve regional, rural and remote communities. The project notes that current reliance on enrolment data can mask real demand in those areas, limiting visibility of the skills needed to support critical industries. The study will explore whether alternative data sources and emerging technologies can fill this gap.
