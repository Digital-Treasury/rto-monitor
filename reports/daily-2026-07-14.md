### TEQSA News

**Joint sector alert issued for overseas student transfers**
*Published: 2026-07-13 | Relevance: HIGH*

TEQSA and ASQA have issued a joint sector alert flagging compliance concerns about how some providers are advertising in relation to onshore overseas student transfers. The alert targets providers whose arrangements may be inconsistent with the ban on education agent commissions for facilitating transfers — a ban intended to stop agents steering students into unnecessary moves for financial gain. Both regulators expect providers to review their processes now; those found non-compliant may face a compliance assessment or regulatory action.

**Amendments to the Threshold Standards**
*Published: 2026-07-14 | Relevance: HIGH*

The Australian Government has introduced amendments to the Higher Education Standards Framework (Threshold Standards) 2021, covering racism and governance. The instrument is already on the Federal Register of Legislation with specific effective dates. This applies to dual-sector providers — RTOs that are also registered as higher education providers — and TEQSA has flagged it will provide sector support in coming months.
