### ASIC Newsroom

**ASIC proposes to remake qualified accountant legislative instrument**
*Published: 2026-07-15 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

ASIC is seeking feedback on a proposal to remake the ASIC Corporations (Qualified Accountant) Instrument 2016/786, which expires 1 October 2026. The instrument defines which professional body members can be recognised as "qualified accountants" for regulatory purposes. ASIC proposes to extend it for 10 years with minor clarifying amendments — the practical effect does not change. RTOs offering accounting and financial services qualifications should note the expiry date is covered; no action required unless they wish to submit feedback.

Content-worthy: NO
