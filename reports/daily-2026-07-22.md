### ITECA News

**Australia's Skills System Held Together By Independent Sector**
*Published: 2026-07-22 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

ITECA reports on NCVER's release of Total VET Activity data for 2025, which shows the independent skills training sector delivers the majority of VET activity in Australia. ### ASIC Newsroom

**Federal Court finds former Noumi CEO breached directors' duties and financial reporting obligations**
*Published: 2026-07-22 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

The Federal Court has found that Rory Macleod, former managing director and CEO of Noumi Limited, contravened the Corporations Act in relation to financial reporting for the year ended 30 June 2019 and the half-year ended 31 December 2019. The finding relates to directors' duties around accurate financial reporting. As most RTOs operate as companies, their owners and directors are subject to the same Corporations Act obligations — this case is a reminder that inaccurate financial reporting can result in personal liability, independent of the company's regulatory status.
