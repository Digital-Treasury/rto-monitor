## Lead stories this week

### 1. CRICOS application suspension (HIGH)

**Suspension on new CRICOS applications**
*Source: ASQA Latest News | Published: 2026-05-19 | Captured: 2026-05-26*

The Australian Government introduced a temporary suspension on certain new applications under the ESOS Act, covering CRICOS registration for new providers and course additions. RTOs planning to submit new CRICOS applications or add courses for international students should pause those plans until the suspension is lifted. No end date has been confirmed. Existing CRICOS registrations are unaffected.

URL: https://www.asqa.gov.au/node/674

### 2. Tribunal upholds Gills College qualification cancellation (HIGH)

**Tribunal decision affirms cancellation of Gills College qualifications**
*Source: ASQA Media Releases | Published: 2026-05-27 | Captured: 2026-05-28*

The Administrative Review Tribunal upheld ASQA's decision to cancel qualifications issued by Gills College, continuing a consistent pattern of tribunal decisions that affirm ASQA's enforcement powers. Once ASQA cancels qualifications and the decision survives review, there is no further avenue for delivery. RTO directors should understand that non-compliance — especially at the qualification integrity level — carries permanent operational consequences.

URL: https://www.asqa.gov.au/node/683

---

### 3. ATEC inaugural Chief Commissioner appointed (MEDIUM)

**ITECA welcomes appointment of ATEC Commissioners**
*Source: ITECA News | Published: 2026-05-28 | Captured: 2026-05-29*

Professor Barney Glover AO has been appointed inaugural Chief Commissioner of the Australian Tertiary Education Commission (ATEC), stepping across from his role as JSA Commissioner. The ATEC consolidates oversight of both VET and higher education. With a Commissioner now in place, ATEC is operationally active. RTOs — particularly dual-sector providers — should begin monitoring ATEC outputs alongside ASQA guidance, as funding and policy signals will increasingly route through the new body.

URL: https://www.iteca.edu.au/news/ITECA%20welcomes%20appointment%20of%20ATEC%20Commissioners.aspx
