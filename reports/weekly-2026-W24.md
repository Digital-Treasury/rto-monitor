**Source:** Skills Insight | **Published:** 2026-06-09 | **Relevance:** MEDIUM

### 2. Updated Certificate II in Rural Operations open for final comment

**Source:** Skills Insight | **Published:** 2026-06-09 | **Relevance:** MEDIUM

The Certificate II in Rural Operations has been revised — including a title change to resolve ambiguity about "rural" limiting the qualification to inland contexts. The updated qualification is now open for final stakeholder review. A Consensus Gathering Workshop is available for those wanting to understand the changes before submitting input. RTOs delivering this qualification should review and respond before the window closes.

URL: https://skillsinsight.com.au/news/updated-certificate-ii-in-rural-operations-available-for-comment/
