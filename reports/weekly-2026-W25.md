## Top items this week

### 1. JSA paper calls for skills-first approach to tertiary education

**Source:** Jobs and Skills Australia | **Published:** 2026-06-17 | **Relevance:** MEDIUM

Jobs and Skills Australia published a paper arguing for stronger alignment between skills and qualifications to support lifelong learning. The paper frames tertiary education reform around skills mobility and productivity — qualifications should more directly map to demonstrated skills. This signals where national VET policy is heading and is likely to shape funding prioritisation in future cycles. RTOs that can articulate their qualifications in skills-mobility terms will be better placed for the next funding review.

URL: https://www.jobsandskills.gov.au/news/new-paper-calls-skills-first-approach-tertiary-education
