## Top items this week

### 1. ASQA — Quality Indicator Summary Reports due TODAY (30 June)
*Published: 2026-06-19 | Relevance: HIGH*

ASQA issued a reminder that quality indicator summary reports must be submitted by 30 June 2026 — today is the deadline. This is a mandatory annual obligation under the Standards for RTOs. Any RTO that has not yet lodged faces regulatory risk. The deadline has not been extended.

URL: https://www.asqa.gov.au/node/699

### 2. ITECA — Australia's Apprenticeship and Trainee Pipeline Continues to Shrink
*Published: 2026-06-23 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

New data shows apprenticeship and traineeship commencements are falling at a time when workforce demand across construction, manufacturing, and trades remains high. For RTOs delivering apprenticeship programs, the decline translates directly to fewer enrolments and reduced funding. ITECA frames this as a structural policy problem, not a cyclical dip.

URL: https://www.iteca.edu.au/web/news/Articles/Australia's%20Apprentice%20and%20Trainee%20Pipeline%20Continues%20to%20Shrink%20at%20a%20Critical%20Time.aspx

---

### 3. TEQSA — New gen AI assessment resource
*Published: 2026-06-24 | Relevance: MEDIUM*

TEQSA published the third in its assessment reform series, focused on assuring learning quality in a gen AI-integrated environment. The framework centres on building student adaptive capabilities — evaluative judgement, critical thinking, ethical reasoning — as both quality assurance and workforce preparation. Dual-sector RTOs are directly in scope; the principles apply broadly to any VET provider revising assessment design.

URL: https://www.teqsa.gov.au/about-us/news-and-events/latest-news/new-resource-gen-ai-and-assurances-learning
