## Lead items

**1. ASQA establishes Provider Reference Group (HIGH)**
ASQA created a formal Provider Reference Group (PRG) on 9 July to structure engagement between the regulator and the VET sector. RTOs should watch for PRG membership details and opportunities to participate or submit through the group. This is a new formal channel for sector-regulator dialogue — significant for RTOs wanting influence over future regulatory direction.
Source: ASQA Latest News | URL: https://www.asqa.gov.au/node/705

**2. ASIC enforcement on financial adviser qualifications (MEDIUM)**
ASIC reviewed the Financial Advisers Register and found advisers with unrecorded qualifications. 26 advisers had their authority to give personal advice ceased. RTOs delivering FNS qualifications should ensure graduates understand the obligation to have qualifications recorded on the FAR with their AFS licensee — non-compliance has real consequences.
Source: ASIC Newsroom | URL: https://www.asic.gov.au/about-asic/news-centre/news-items/asic-issues-update-on-compliance-with-the-financial-adviser-qualifications-standard/

**3. Six new training package projects approved (MEDIUM)**
The Department of Employment and Workplace Relations approved six new Skills Insight training package development projects to commence late 2026 or early 2027. RTOs in agriculture, textiles, manufacturing and related sectors should identify which projects overlap with their scope and engage in consultations ahead of qualification changes.
Source: Skills Insight | URL: https://skillsinsight.com.au/news/new-skills-insight-projects-2/

**4. Australia's international education competitiveness in question (MEDIUM)**
ITECA CEO Felix Pirie argues Australia's international education sector is underperforming relative to its potential. With July 1 visa application charge increases still fresh, CRICOS-registered RTOs face a tightening student pipeline on top of existing policy constraints.
Source: ITECA News | URL: https://www.iteca.edu.au/web/news/Articles/How%20Australia%20is%20losing%20at%20international%20education.aspx
